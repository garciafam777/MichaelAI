## 🎯 What is Michael AI?

Michael AI is a **browser extension** that lets you:

- Activate the AI instantly through **voice command** or a simple **toggle**.
- Compose and send messages to your contacts, groups, or "your people" (family, friends, community, prayer circle, etc.).
- Use natural voice input for fast, hands-free messaging.
- Rely on intelligent assistance to help phrase your thoughts clearly and warmly.

It’s designed to feel like having a helpful messenger at your side — always ready when inspiration or the need to reach out strikes.

### Key Features
- **Voice Activation** — Say a custom wake phrase to start instantly (no typing needed).
- **Toggle Mode** — One-click activation via browser toolbar or keyboard shortcut.
- **Intelligent Messaging** — AI helps refine, suggest, or complete your message while preserving your authentic voice.
- **Privacy-Focused** — Messages stay between you and your intended recipients (details in Privacy section).
- **Seamless Browser Integration** — Works while you browse, work, or reflect.
- **Customizable** — Choose your preferred contacts/groups and activation style.

*(More features will be added based on tester feedback during this initial phase.)*

## 🚀 How to Install & Get Started (Testing Phase)

1. **Download the extension** from the testing link / GitHub release / Chrome Web Store (when published).
2. **Install** it in your browser (Chrome, Edge, or other Chromium-based browsers recommended).
3. **Grant necessary permissions** (microphone for voice, access to messaging platforms you choose).
4. **Set up your "People"** — Add contacts or groups you want to message easily.
5. **Configure Activation**:
   - Choose your voice wake phrase (example: "Hey Michael" or "Send message").
   - Or enable the toolbar toggle.
6. **Start testing!**  
   Speak or type your message → Michael AI helps deliver it.

**Note:** This is an early testing version. Some features may be limited or still evolving. Bugs are expected — your feedback is incredibly valuable.

## 🗣️ How to Use

### Voice Mode
- Activate with your chosen wake phrase.
- Speak naturally: "Tell Mom I'm thinking of her and to have a blessed day."
- Michael AI will confirm, refine if needed, and help send.

### Toggle Mode
- Click the Michael AI icon in your browser toolbar.
- Type or continue with voice.
- Review and send.

### Example Messages
- Encouragement: "Just wanted to say I'm proud of you."
- Prayer: "Praying for healing and peace over your situation."
- Quick Update: "Made it home safely — love you."
